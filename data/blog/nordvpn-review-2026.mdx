## Key Features

NordVPN distinguishes itself by offering a suite of security-focused features designed to tackle modern cyber threats. According to NordVPN documentation, these features operate both on the network level and within the application itself.

### NordLynx Protocol

NordLynx is NordVPN's proprietary tunneling protocol, built around the modern WireGuard architecture. Traditionally, VPNs relied on protocols like OpenVPN, which are secure but can be resource-intensive and slower to establish connections.

NordLynx was designed to prioritize performance without sacrificing security. By utilizing WireGuard's streamlined code base, NordLynx reduces connection overhead significantly. This translates to faster server connection times and better sustained speeds, which is particularly noticeable when streaming high-definition video or downloading large files.

Furthermore, NordLynx offers better efficiency on mobile devices. Because it requires less processing power to encrypt and decrypt data packets, it is less demanding on smartphone batteries compared to older protocols. This makes NordLynx one of NordVPN's key differentiators, providing a practical balance between robust encryption and everyday usability.

### Threat Protection

A notable addition to NordVPN is its Threat Protection feature. Unlike a standard VPN connection, Threat Protection acts similarly to a lightweight antivirus. It blocks websites known for phishing, stops intrusive web trackers, and scans downloaded files for malware before they execute on your device.

### Meshnet

Meshnet is a practical networking tool that changes how you interact with devices remotely. It allows you to create a private, encrypted network between your personal devices, regardless of their physical location. Instead of connecting to a remote NordVPN server, Meshnet lets you route traffic directly through your home desktop from your smartphone while traveling.

### Double VPN

For users operating in higher-risk environments, NordVPN offers Double VPN. This feature routes your traffic through two separate VPN servers instead of one, encrypting your data twice. While this does decrease connection speeds due to the encryption overhead, it provides an additional layer of anonymity.

### Dedicated IP

When using a standard VPN, you share an IP address with many other users. This can occasionally trigger security CAPTCHAs or lead to the IP being flagged by certain services. NordVPN allows you to purchase a Dedicated IP address—a static IP address assigned exclusively to you. This is useful for accessing restricted corporate databases or whitelisting your connection.

### Split Tunneling

Split Tunneling allows you to choose which applications are routed through the secure VPN tunnel and which connect directly to your local internet service provider (ISP). For example, you can route your web browser through the VPN for privacy while allowing a local banking application to connect directly to avoid location-based security alerts.

### Kill Switch

A responsive kill switch is a standard failsafe for VPNs. If your connection to the NordVPN server drops unexpectedly, the kill switch severs your device's internet connection. This prevents your true IP address and unencrypted web traffic from leaking to your ISP during the disconnect.

## Security and Privacy

A Virtual Private Network is a security product at its core. Let's examine the specific cryptographic protocols and privacy policies NordVPN utilizes to protect user data.

### Encryption Standards

NordVPN utilizes AES-256-GCM encryption, which is widely used as a standard in the cybersecurity industry. This is the same level of encryption used by governments and financial institutions to protect sensitive data. Brute-forcing AES-256 encryption using current computing power is not considered feasible.

### No-Logs Policy

A "no-logs" policy is central to VPN privacy. It means the provider states they do not track, record, or store identifiable details about your online activities. This includes the websites you visit, the files you download, or your connection timestamps. NordVPN's privacy policy explicitly outlines this commitment.

### Independent Audits

NordVPN does not rely solely on internal claims. Independent audits have verified that the company's server infrastructure aligns with its privacy policies. Regular assessments by firms like Deloitte confirm that their transition to diskless RAM-only servers effectively prevents long-term data storage.

### Jurisdiction

NordVPN is incorporated and headquartered in Panama. Panama has privacy-friendly laws and does not require technology companies to retain user data. Furthermore, Panama operates outside the jurisdiction of major intelligence-sharing alliances like the Five Eyes, providing a reliable legal shield for user privacy.

### DNS Leak Protection

Occasionally, a configuration issue in an operating system might send DNS requests outside the encrypted VPN tunnel, exposing browsing history to the ISP. NordVPN addresses this by including built-in DNS leak protection. Combining this network protection with secure credential tools like [Bitwarden](/blog/bitwarden-review-2026) or [1Password](/blog/1password-review-2026) helps maintain overall privacy.

## Streaming Performance

For many users, the ability to access geo-restricted content is a primary reason for using a VPN. Based on our testing, NordVPN performs consistently in this category.

### Netflix

Streaming services employ sophisticated anti-VPN blocking technology. NordVPN utilizes SmartPlay technology to bypass these restrictions. During testing in 2026, NordVPN unblocked regional Netflix libraries—including the US, UK, Japan, and Canada catalogs—allowing for stable 4K streaming without significant buffering.

### Disney+

Similar to Netflix, Disney+ restricts content based on physical location due to licensing agreements. NordVPN handles Disney's geographic restrictions reliably, granting access to international libraries like Disney+ Star with standard load times and consistent streaming quality.

### Amazon Prime Video

Amazon Prime Video is often difficult to unblock, as it actively flags commercial VPN IP addresses. However, NordVPN’s broad server network means that if one server is blocked, users can disconnect and switch to another. We found success accessing the US and UK Prime Video catalogs during our tests.

### BBC iPlayer

For accessing British television, NordVPN is a practical choice. By connecting to one of its UK-based servers, users can bypass the geo-blocks implemented by BBC iPlayer. This allows for streaming of live news and standard programming from outside the United Kingdom.

### Travel Use Cases

When traveling internationally, home streaming subscriptions are often unavailable. NordVPN allows travelers to connect back to servers in their home country, restoring access to local news networks, regional banking apps, and customized streaming libraries.

## Apps and Ease of Use

A cybersecurity tool is only practical if it is straightforward to use. NordVPN has designed its applications to be accessible for general users while retaining advanced settings.

### Windows

The NordVPN Windows application is well-established. Its primary interface features an interactive world map that allows users to connect to a specific country by clicking a pin. For users who prefer a standard layout, a sidebar provides access to server lists and specialized options.

### macOS

NordVPN offers two versions of its macOS application, both providing a similar map-based interface. The macOS app integrates with Apple's operating system, running in the background without drawing excessive system resources or heavily impacting battery life.

### Android

The Android application translates the desktop experience to mobile devices. It retains the map interface, optimized for touch controls. It includes the majority of features found on desktop versions, including split tunneling, which is particularly useful for separating web traffic from local banking apps.

### iPhone and iPad

Apple’s iOS development guidelines limit what third-party VPN apps can do within the system, but NordVPN provides a functional app for iOS devices. While it lacks deep split tunneling, it utilizes the efficient NordLynx protocol to provide stable connections and a clean interface.

### Browser Extensions

For users who only want to encrypt web browser traffic, NordVPN offers lightweight extensions for Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. These extensions act as secure proxies, changing the visible IP address and blocking intrusive ads without affecting other applications on the computer.

Both NordVPN and Surfshark offer industry-standard privacy protections using RAM-only servers. NordVPN's jurisdiction in Panama is often viewed as slightly more privacy-friendly than Surfshark's base in the Netherlands. You can also explore options like [Proton VPN](/blog/proton-vpn-review-2026) if Swiss jurisdiction is a priority.

### Features

NordVPN offers proprietary features like Threat Protection and Meshnet. Surfshark counters with its CleanWeb ad-blocking feature and offers broader device compatibility.

### Pricing

Surfshark is generally the lower-cost option. It is cheaper on long-term plans and allows for unlimited simultaneous device connections, whereas NordVPN caps accounts at 10 devices.

### Best Choice for Different Users

- **Choose NordVPN** if you prioritize connection speeds, streaming reliability, and specialized security tools.
- **Choose Surfshark** if you are on a strict budget or need to connect a large number of devices simultaneously.
