## What Is Proton VPN?

Understanding the foundation of a cybersecurity tool is critical when entrusting it with your private data. Proton VPN is not merely a standalone product; it is part of a broader ecosystem dedicated to secure communications.

### Company Background

Proton VPN was developed by the team behind ProtonMail, the world's largest encrypted email service. The project originated in 2014 from scientists who met at CERN (the European Organization for Nuclear Research). Driven by concerns regarding mass surveillance and internet censorship, they launched Proton VPN as a crowdfunded initiative. This scientific background heavily influences the company's approach to product development, prioritizing cryptographic integrity and verifiable security over marketing hyperbole.

### Proton Ecosystem

In 2026, subscribing to Proton VPN often means integrating into the wider Proton ecosystem. The company has expanded its offerings to include Proton Drive (secure cloud storage), Proton Calendar, and Proton Pass (a specialized secure credential tool that competes favorably alongside the [best password managers](/blog/best-password-managers-2026)). ### Reputation and Transparency

In the cybersecurity industry, reputation is paramount. Proton VPN has cultivated a reputation as a staunch defender of digital privacy. The company actively participates in legal challenges against overreaching government surveillance laws and regularly publishes transparency reports. This commitment to fighting for user rights has made it the preferred tool for activists and journalists working in hostile digital environments.

### Independent Audits

## Why Proton VPN Stands Out

In a saturated market, Proton VPN differentiates itself not through aggressive marketing, but through fundamental architectural choices. Here is an in-depth look at why it stands out from the competition.

### Swiss Jurisdiction

Proton VPN is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, a nation renowned for its robust privacy laws. Crucially, Switzerland is not a member of the Fourteen Eyes intelligence-sharing agreement and operates outside EU and US data retention mandates. Under Swiss law, compelling a company to perform targeted logging requires a valid Swiss court order. Because Proton VPN utilizes a verified no-logs infrastructure, it inherently lacks the historical user data that could be surrendered, providing a legally robust layer of privacy protection.

### Open-Source Applications

While many VPN providers rely on proprietary, closed-source code, Proton VPN has open-sourced all of its desktop and mobile applications. This allows the underlying code to be publicly inspected on platforms like GitHub. This public code review fosters community trust, enabling independent security researchers to actively search for vulnerabilities and verify that no hidden backdoors exist.

### Secure Core Architecture

Secure Core mitigates advanced network-based attacks through sophisticated multi-hop privacy routing. When activated, user traffic is initially routed through highly secure, Proton-owned servers located in privacy-friendly jurisdictions before reaching the final destination server. Consequently, even if the exit server is compromised, an adversary can only trace the connection back to the Secure Core server, entirely obscuring the user's true IP address and providing vital protection for high-risk threat models.

### Independent Security Audits

Proton VPN relies on empirical evidence rather than marketing claims. The company regularly submits its applications and infrastructure to comprehensive independent security audits by respected third-party firms like SEC Consult. By publishing the full results of these penetration tests and code analyses on their website, Proton VPN allows users to see exactly what vulnerabilities were identified and how they were patched, transforming security claims into verifiable facts.

## Key Features

Beyond its foundational architecture, Proton VPN offers a comprehensive suite of practical features designed to enhance daily digital security and usability.

### Secure Core

Secure Core routes traffic through privacy-friendly jurisdictions to mitigate network-based attacks, as detailed previously in this review.

### NetShield

NetShield is Proton VPN’s integrated ad, malware, and tracker blocker. Operating at the DNS level, it actively filters out malicious domains and prevents intrusive tracking scripts from loading. This not only protects users from phishing attempts and targeted advertising but also significantly improves page load times and reduces unnecessary bandwidth consumption.

### VPN Accelerator

VPN Accelerator is a set of proprietary technologies that Proton VPN states can improve connection speeds, particularly over long distances. By overcoming CPU limitations, redesigning VPN protocols, and utilizing advanced networking techniques, this feature ensures that users maintain reliable connections even when accessing geographically distant servers.

### Kill Switch

A robust kill switch is a mandatory feature for any privacy-focused tool. Proton VPN’s kill switch continuously monitors the connection status. If the encrypted tunnel drops unexpectedly, the kill switch instantly blocks all network traffic, ensuring that the user’s true IP address and unencrypted data are never exposed to their local network or ISP.

### Split Tunneling

Split tunneling allows users to define exactly which applications or IP addresses route through the secure VPN tunnel and which connect directly to the internet. This is highly useful for accessing local network devices, utilizing domestic banking applications that restrict foreign IPs, or optimizing speeds for low-risk gaming while keeping web browsing strictly encrypted.

### WireGuard Support

Proton VPN fully integrates WireGuard, the modern standard for secure networking protocols. WireGuard features a highly streamlined codebase that allows for rapid connection times and efficient performance. It delivers reliable speeds for streaming and large file transfers while maintaining state-of-the-art cryptographic security, making it the preferred protocol for most daily applications.

### Tor Over VPN

For users requiring access to the deep web, Proton VPN offers specialized servers that integrate seamlessly with the Tor anonymity network. With a single click, users can route all their internet traffic through the Tor network, granting access to Onion sites without needing to install the dedicated Tor browser, providing an additional layer of routing obfuscation.

### Open-Source Apps

As discussed, all applications are fully open-source, allowing for public code review and increased community trust.

## Security and Privacy

At its core, Proton VPN is engineered to provide strict digital security. The implementation of its cryptographic standards reflects a commitment to data protection.

### Encryption Standards

According to Proton documentation, Proton VPN employs AES-256 encryption, the global gold standard for securing sensitive data. This cipher is currently utilized by military organizations and financial institutions worldwide. In addition, the service uses 4096-bit RSA key exchange and HMAC with SHA384 for message authentication. Crucially, Proton VPN implements Perfect Forward Secrecy, meaning that a new encryption key is generated for every session. Even if an adversary were to capture encrypted traffic and somehow obtain a future key, they could not retroactively decrypt past communications.

### No-Logs Policy

The foundation of Proton VPN’s privacy promise is a strictly enforced no-logs policy. The company states unequivocally that it does not monitor, record, or store user browsing history, DNS queries, connection timestamps, or IP addresses. Because this data is never collected, it cannot be compromised in a server breach or surrendered to government authorities, ensuring complete anonymity regarding user activity.

### Jurisdiction

Operating under Swiss jurisdiction provides the service with strong legal protections against forced data retention, separating it from Fourteen Eyes alliances.

### Independent Audits

Continuous independent audits verify adherence to privacy commitments, transforming security claims into proven facts accessible via public transparency reports.

### Transparency Reports

To maintain absolute transparency, Proton VPN publishes regular transparency reports detailing the number of legal requests for user data they receive from authorities. Because of their no-logs architecture, they are consistently unable to provide any meaningful data regarding user activity, reinforcing the practical effectiveness of their privacy infrastructure.

### Threat Model Considerations

Proton VPN is designed to defend against advanced threat models, including nation-state surveillance and sophisticated network monitoring. While average users benefit from this high level of security, it is specifically engineered to protect journalists, activists, and researchers who operate in environments where digital privacy is a matter of physical safety.
